Deep Cleaning Services

Experience the ultimate in cleanliness with our comprehensive deep cleaning service. We tackle every corner of your home to ensure it’s spotless and fresh.

Our Deep Cleaning Includes:

General Cleaning in All Rooms

  • Thorough dry dusting of walls
  • Vacuuming and wiping of all surfaces, including carpentry, radiators, and the tops of wardrobes and cabinets
  • Meticulous dusting of lamps, shelves, and picture frames
  • Careful dusting of electrical sockets
  • Vacuuming upholstered furniture
  • Vacuuming under and around rugs (where feasible)
  • Gentle dusting of electronics (excluding screens)
  • Shining mirrors to a streak-free finish
  • Emptying and sanitizing rubbish bins
  • Vacuuming and mopping all floors

Kitchen

  • Deep clean of the kitchen fan and fan filter (excluding the carbon filter)
  • Wiping down the tops of all cabinets and shelves
  • Cleaning both the interior and exterior of kitchen doors
  • Scrubbing the exterior of the fridge and freezer
  • Cleaning the tiles and splash guard above the sink
  • Cleaning under cabinets (where possible) and wiping bases
  • Cleaning both inside and out of the microwave
  • Thorough cleaning of the dishwasher, including the interior, exterior, rubber strip, detergent compartment, and filter
  • Wiping clean kitchen appliances, including coffee machines, oil bottles, and salt and pepper shakers
  • Cleaning the inside of the top cutlery drawer
  • Cleaning the cabinet where the rubbish bin is located, inside and out
  • Emptying the rubbish and cleaning the bin
  • Polishing the sink, faucet, stopper, and strainer

Bathrooms

  • Cleaning and sanitizing walls and floors
  • Polishing faucets, pipes, and shower nozzles
  • Degreasing and descaling shower walls and joints where possible
  • Cleaning the shower or bathtub (note: customer must remove the bathtub front)
  • Cleaning the floor drain
  • Cleaning the exterior of bathroom cabinets
  • Cleaning the faucet and basin (excluding water lock)
  • Cleaning the toilet thoroughly
  • Cleaning the exterior of bathroom appliances and the washing machine’s detergent holder
  • Cleaning the tumble dryer filter
  • Wiping off soap bottles

Bedrooms

  • Making the beds neatly
  • Dusting or vacuuming the headboard and bed frame

Hallways

  • Dusting handrails, banisters, and stair rails
  • Vacuuming and wiping shoe racks
  • Wiping the coat rack
  • Wiping down the fuse cabinet
