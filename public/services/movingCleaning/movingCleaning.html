Move-Out Cleaning Services

Moving out can be overwhelming, but cleaning up doesn’t have to be. Let us take the stress out of leaving your old home spotless and ready for its next occupants.

What Our Move-Out Cleaning Includes:

Complete General Cleaning

  • Thorough window cleaning to make them sparkle
  • Detailed vacuuming and wiping of all surfaces, including carpentry, skirting boards, doors, and windowsills
  • Refreshing wardrobes, cabinets, and bookshelves, inside and out
  • Cleaning floor rails for sliding doors and radiators
  • Dusting of walls, electrical sockets, and lamps
  • Polishing mirrors for a crystal-clear finish
  • Removing ash from stoves and fireplaces
  • Wiping down front doors
  • Vacuuming and mopping all floors to ensure they shine

Kitchen Care

  • Deep cleaning of the fridge and freezer, inside and out
  • Cleaning the stove, oven, plates, and grilles to remove any residue
  • Cleaning the kitchen fan and filter (excluding the carbon filter)
  • Wiping down cupboards, shelves, and drawers, both inside and out
  • Scrubbing tiles and splash guards above the sink
  • Cleaning under cabinets and wiping their bases
  • Refreshing the microwave and dishwasher, including the interior, exterior, and filters
  • Emptying and sanitizing the rubbish bin
  • Polishing the sink, faucet, stopper, and strainer

Bathroom Refresh

  • Cleaning and sanitizing walls and floors
  • Polishing faucets, pipes, and shower nozzles
  • Degreasing and descaling shower walls and joints where possible
  • Cleaning the floor drain and shower or bathtub (note: customer must remove the bathtub front)
  • Cleaning the interior and exterior of bathroom cabinets
  • Refreshing the faucet, basin, and toilet
  • Cleaning the exterior of bathroom appliances and the washing machine’s detergent holder
  • Cleaning the tumble dryer filter

Hallways

  • Dusting handrails, banisters, and stair rails
  • Wiping down coat racks and the fuse cabinet

Window Cleaning

  • Thorough cleaning of all window panes, inside and out
  • Cleaning skylights (if they turn on their axis; roof access not included)
  • All necessary window cleaning equipment and solutions provided
  • Note: Glazed balconies and sunrooms are not included but can be scheduled separately

Why Choose Our Move-Out Cleaning?

  • Professional, reliable cleaning staff
  • Convenient scheduling options to fit your move timeline
  • Use of eco-friendly cleaning products
  • Transparent and competitive pricing
