Window Cleaning Services

At Vi4Dig Service AB, we provide top-notch window cleaning services designed to enhance the clarity and appearance of your windows. Our professional team ensures that every pane is spotless, offering a streak-free finish that brightens up your home or office.

Our window cleaning service includes:

  • Thorough Cleaning: We clean both the interior and exterior sides of your windows for a complete shine.
  • Skylight Cleaning: We handle skylights that are accessible and can be cleaned from within; however, we cannot service skylights that require roof access.
  • Equipment and Cleaning Agents: We provide all necessary cleaning equipment and eco-friendly agents to ensure a thorough job.
  • Additional Ladder Service: If you do not have a suitable ladder, we can bring one for an additional fee. Please inform us when booking.
  • Frame Washing: For an extra touch of cleanliness, we recommend adding a frame washer service to clean window frames and sills. Specify this option when booking if desired.

What Counts as a Window?

Our pricing is based on the number of window frames, or sashes, you have:

  • Single Frame: A window with one frame (sash) counts as one window.
  • Double Frame: A window with two frames (one sliding right and one left) counts as two windows.
